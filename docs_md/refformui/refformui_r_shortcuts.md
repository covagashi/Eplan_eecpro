# Shortcuts

This functionality is only available for certain module packages. [Info / Copyright](license.htm) You are here:  Shortcuts By means of the ampersand (&amp; or &#38;) **shortcuts** are specified, to ensure the usability of the Form-UI in absence of the mouse. Shortcuts are **underlined characters** of a word, which is displayed in a menu or a button. The combination of the [Alt] key and the underlined letter will trigger the desired function, according to a mouse clickon the menu item or button. In addition a button should be used by the shortcut [Alt] \+ [S]: [Example](javascript:void\(0\);)
    
        <open type="button" receiver="mos('Station')" ref="doku" >Go to **& amp;**Station</open>
