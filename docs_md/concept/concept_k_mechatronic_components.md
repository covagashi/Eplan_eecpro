## Mechatronic components

**Mechatronic components** are the basis for an interdisciplinary, modular process. The are the center of **mechatronical development processes** and decouple the task of the discipline-spanning **development** of mechatronic components from the **configuration** with mechatronic components.

[](../Pictures/2006102018.png)

The goal is to avoid copying from project to project (-> Wall) and to minimize project-specific efforts.

Reusable components are developed by analyzing existing projects. In the example the development team has recognized the gripper as a component that is used multiple times through looking at the completed projects and has included it into the modular system.
