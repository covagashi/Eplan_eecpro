# Test 5 - Checking the arrangement of the slave nodes

This functionality is only available for certain module packages. [Info / Copyright](license.htm) You are here:  Test 5 - Checking the arrangement of the slave nodes A check should be carried out whether the devices and persons in the rooms are labeled, and whether on the devices, in addition, LAN jacks are arranged as slaves.
    1. In the Project Catalog, open the diagram IT_SolutionMap with a double-click.
In the diagram, the names or numbers are shown as labels on the upper edge of the nodes for persons and devices (see Point 1 in the above figure). The LAN jacks of the devices are also shown as labels (see Point 2 in the above figure).
    1. Move the LAN-jack of PC Macroeconomix from the left-hand side to the right.
    2. When aligning, follow the displayed blue help line.
A help line is always displayed when the object to be moved is centrally situated, or with an outside edge in alignment with another object.
    1. Close the diagram without saving the changes.
