## ECAD

The ECAD discipline serves to create schematic fragments consisting of page and window macros. The schematic fragments are allocated to the mechatronical components of the modular system. A discipline-specific concept is defined by means of the Plug-Socket concept.

An ECAD discipline structure is created from the configuration by means of a command. From the resulting ECAD structure the schematics are created, also by means of a command.
