## Example: Creation of a page for light

Initial situation: Schematic page with a maximum of 5 lamps.

The variance in this example is the number of lamps. Thus, the page is broken down into the following essential elements:

     * Basic wiring diagram with fuse and contact
     * A variant (A) of a single lamp with its terminals and connections via T-pieces.
     * A variant (B) of a single lamp with its terminals and connections via angles for the last connection.

In the placement of sockets and plugs, the relative position to the graphic is very important. It is therefore recommended that all sockets be placed in the entire circuit, and to derive the individual macros from this by copying. Generally, the alignment of the plug and socket texts is irrelevant. Only the location of their extension points matters.
