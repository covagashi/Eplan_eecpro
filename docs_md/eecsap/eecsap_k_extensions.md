## Extensions

The SAP_BusinessObjects library is not part of the scope of supply of the SAP interface. It can also be inherited.

Various examples and templates are included therein from the following areas:

     * Material
     * Material parts lists
     * Customer order parts lists
     * Customer order
     * Document info set
     * Change number
     * Classification

These templates can be used to quickly and easily adapt standard queries to customer-specific requirements.
