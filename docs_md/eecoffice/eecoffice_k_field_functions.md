## Field functions

The use of fields is supported in main and fragment documents.

Fields whose contents are collections of other fields, for example TOC and Index, have to be updated after the generation of the document (see [Word.UpdateWordFieldsCommand](refcommands_r_word_updatefieldscommand.htm)). The updating always refers to the total of all fields that are contained in the created document.
